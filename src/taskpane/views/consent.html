Data Sharing Consent

To provide you with the best AI-powered financial modeling experience, we need your consent to share certain data with our trusted partners.

What data is shared?

  • Financial model parameters and assumptions
  • Business planning and valuation data
  • Anonymized usage patterns for model improvement

Why is this data shared?

We share this data with our AI partners to:

  • Enhance model planning algorithms and accuracy
  • Improve financial data encoding and processing
  • Develop better industry-specific templates
  • Ensure compliance with financial modeling standards

Your privacy matters

All shared data is anonymized and aggregated. No personally identifiable information or sensitive business details are shared with third parties.
